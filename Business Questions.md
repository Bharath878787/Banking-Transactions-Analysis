## Business Questions

This project is designed to answer the following key business questions:

1. What is the total number of transactions processed daily, monthly, quarterly, and yearly?  
2. What is the total transaction amount across different branches?  
3. What is the average transaction value per customer?  
4. Which customers have the highest transaction volume or value?  
5. What percentage of customers are repeat users?  
6. Which transaction types (Credit, Debit, ATM withdrawal, Online transfer) are most common?  
7. Which branches contribute the most to overall revenue?  
8. How do transaction volumes vary across regions?  
9. Which branch has the highest average transaction per customer?  
10. Which channel (UPI, Online, Branch, Mobile) has the highest success rate?  
11. What percentage of transactions are flagged as failed, reversed, or suspicious?  
12. How is transaction revenue growing month over month?  
13. Which merchants (Amazon, Swiggy, Uber, Flipkart, etc.) generate the most revenue?  
14. Which customer segments (by account type, city, or bank) contribute most to revenue?
